Instructions

Overview

Your job is to monitor security cameras and report any anomalies you observe. Stay vigilant and report anything suspicious!

Controls

Use 1-9 to switch between cameras

Press Space to open the report menu

Use ESC to pause the game

Reporting Anomalies

1. When you notice something unusual, open the report menu

2. Select the camera where you spotted the anomaly

3. Choose the correct type of anomaly from the list

4. Submit your report

Tips

- Pay attention to the initial state of each room

- Watch for sudden camera blackouts

- Keep track of object positions

- Check mirrors regularly

- Monitor any screens or electronic devices

Warning

False reports will reduce your performance score. Make sure you're certain before submitting a report.
